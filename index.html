About the Artist

Rajmati Rajaram Harjan is a visionary sketch artist whose work bridge the gap between reality and the ethereal. With every stroke of her pencil, she seeks to capture the unspoken emotions and the raw beauty of her subjects.

Based on a deep appreciation for classical techniques, her contemporary approach brings a unique perspective to portraits and nature-inspired themes. Her journey is one of continuous exploration, turning blank canvases into windows of the heart.
